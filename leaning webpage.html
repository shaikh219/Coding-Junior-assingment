UPSC (Union Public Service Commission): The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) is India's central recruiting agency responsible for conducting prestigious civil service examinations and selecting candidates for key governmental positions. Established under Article 315 of the Constitution of India, UPSC plays a pivotal role in shaping the country's administrative framework.
